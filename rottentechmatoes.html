Rotten Techmatoes

UI/UX Design

This was a semester-long project for my CS 2340: Objects and Design class. We created an Android app where Georgia Tech students could rate and review movies. The app was developed in Android Studio. Movie data such as new releases, top rentals, and reccommended movies were taken from the Rotten Tomatoes API. User accounts were backed by a Firebase database.

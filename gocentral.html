GoCentral

Frontend Software Development

During my last summer internship, I worked at GoDaddy on the GoCentral team. GoCentral is GoDaddy's website builder tool, with integrated marketing and eCommerce features. It's easy to use and perfect for small business owners.

As a frontend SDE intern, I contributed to several features for their widgets, which ranged from call-to-action buttons to new widget layouts. During the internship, I became familiar with frameworks such as React.js, Bootstrap, and Node.js. While I can't go into too much detail about my work, I encourage you to go to GoCentral and start a free trial to test it out!
